Pig Dice

Instructions

  • This is a two player game.
  • Each player gets a chance to roll the pair of dice.
  • The value of each dice is summed up temporarily.
  • When the player presses hold it saves the current held value into the score sheet.
  • If either player rolls a one on either die the current value is reduced to a zero.
  • The goal is to reach 100 on the score sheet before your opponent.
